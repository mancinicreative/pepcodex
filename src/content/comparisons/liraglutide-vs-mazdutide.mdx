## Overview

Liraglutide and Mazdutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Liraglutide:** The first long-acting GLP-1 receptor agonist, FDA-approved for type 2 diabetes (Victoza, 2010) and chronic weight management (Saxenda, 2014).

**Mazdutide:** A dual GLP-1 and glucagon receptor agonist developed by Innovent/Eli Lilly.
