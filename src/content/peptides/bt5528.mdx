BT5528 is a first-in-class Bicycle Toxin Conjugate combining targeted delivery with cytotoxic payload. Human clinical data supports the proposed mechanisms.

### How It Works (Simplified)

BT5528 works as a precision "smart missile" delivering chemotherapy directly to cancer cells:

<div class="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-4 not-prose my-8">
  <div class="bg-[color:var(--primary-soft)] rounded-lg p-5 border border-[color:var(--primary-border)]">
    <div class="font-semibold text-[color:var(--ink)] mb-2">Target Recognition</div>
    <p class="text-sm text-[color:var(--ink-muted)]">Bicyclic peptide binds with high affinity to EphA2 receptors overexpressed on cancer cells, sparing normal tissues.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="bg-[color:var(--primary-soft)] rounded-lg p-5 border border-[color:var(--primary-border)]">
    <div class="font-semibold text-[color:var(--ink)] mb-2">Internalization</div>
    <p class="text-sm text-[color:var(--ink-muted)]">Cancer cell engulfs the BT5528 molecule through receptor-mediated endocytosis, bringing the payload inside.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="bg-[color:var(--primary-soft)] rounded-lg p-5 border border-[color:var(--primary-border)]">
    <div class="font-semibold text-[color:var(--ink)] mb-2">Payload Release</div>
    <p class="text-sm text-[color:var(--ink-muted)]">Inside the cell, enzymes cleave the linker and release MMAE toxin into the cytoplasm where it can act.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="bg-[color:var(--primary-soft)] rounded-lg p-5 border border-[color:var(--primary-border)]">
    <div class="font-semibold text-[color:var(--ink)] mb-2">Cell Death</div>
    <p class="text-sm text-[color:var(--ink-muted)]">MMAE disrupts microtubules essential for cell division, causing mitotic arrest and programmed cell death.</p>
  </div>
</div>

**Key Research:** The first-in-human Phase I (Bashir B et al., *J Clin Oncol* 2024; [PMID 39231383](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/39231383/)) reported a 6.7% overall response rate and 20% disease control at the recommended Phase 2 dose across advanced solid tumors. [Trial NCT04180371](https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT04180371)

### Important Limitations

- Investigational agent not yet approved by any regulatory agency
- Preliminary efficacy data from small patient numbers; results may change
- Long-term safety data still maturing, particularly for cumulative neuropathy
- Requires EphA2 expression for activity; not all tumors express target
- Optimal patient selection criteria still being refined
