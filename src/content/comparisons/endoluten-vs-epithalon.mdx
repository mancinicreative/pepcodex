## Overview

Endoluten and Epithalon are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Endoluten:** A cytamin-class peptide supplement derived from pineal gland tissue, part of the Russian bioregulator framework.

**Epithalon:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp-Gly) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to activate telomerase and extend lifespan.
