## Overview

Epithalon and Svetinorm are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Epithalon:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp-Gly) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to activate telomerase and extend lifespan.

**Svetinorm:** A cytamin-class peptide supplement derived from liver tissue, part of the Russian bioregulator framework.
