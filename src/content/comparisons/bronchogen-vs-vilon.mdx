## Overview

Bronchogen and Vilon are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Bronchogen:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp-Leu) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson for bronchial and respiratory tissue support.

**Vilon:** A synthetic dipeptide (Lys-Glu) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson as part of the peptide bioregulation framework.
