## Overview

Cagrilintide and Survodutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Cagrilintide:** A long-acting amylin analog developed by Novo Nordisk for weight management.

**Survodutide:** A dual GLP-1/glucagon receptor agonist developed by Boehringer Ingelheim/Zealand Pharma, currently in Phase 3 trials for obesity and MASH.
