## Overview

Cerluten and Pinealon are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Cerluten:** A cytamin-class peptide supplement derived from brain tissue, part of the Russian bioregulator framework.

**Pinealon:** A synthetic tripeptide (Glu-Asp-Arg) developed by Russian researcher Vladimir Khavinson as a 'peptide bioregulator' claimed to support brain function.
