## Overview

Mazdutide and Survodutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Mazdutide:** A dual GLP-1 and glucagon receptor agonist developed by Innovent/Eli Lilly.

**Survodutide:** A dual GLP-1/glucagon receptor agonist developed by Boehringer Ingelheim/Zealand Pharma, currently in Phase 3 trials for obesity and MASH.
