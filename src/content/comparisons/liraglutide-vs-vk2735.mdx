## Overview

Liraglutide and VK2735 are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Liraglutide:** The first long-acting GLP-1 receptor agonist, FDA-approved for type 2 diabetes (Victoza, 2010) and chronic weight management (Saxenda, 2014).

**VK2735:** A dual GLP-1/GIP receptor agonist from Viking Therapeutics with both subcutaneous and oral formulations.
