## Overview

AOD-9604 and Orforglipron are both studied in the peptide research space.

**AOD-9604:** A modified fragment of human growth hormone (amino acids 176-191 with N-terminal tyrosine) studied for fat loss.

**Orforglipron:** The first oral small-molecule (non-peptide) GLP-1 receptor agonist.
