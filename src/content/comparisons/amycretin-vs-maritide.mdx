## Overview

Amycretin and MariTide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Amycretin:** A novel single-molecule dual GLP-1/amylin receptor agonist developed by Novo Nordisk.

**MariTide:** A bispecific peptide-antibody conjugate combining GLP-1 receptor agonism with GIP receptor antagonism.
