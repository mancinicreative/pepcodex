VK2735

A dual GLP-1/GIP receptor agonist from Viking Therapeutics with both subcutaneous and oral formulations. Phase 2 VENTURE trial showed 14.7% weight loss at 13 weeks (SC) and 12.2% (oral). Phase 3 VANQUISH-1 enrollment completed with 4,650 patients.

Overview

Overview

Quick Summary

  • Dual agonist of GLP-1 and GIP receptors (similar mechanism to tirzepatide)
  • Two formulations: Subcutaneous (weekly) AND oral tablet
  • VENTURE Phase 2 (SC): 14.7% weight loss at 13 weeks with no plateau
  • VENTURE-Oral Phase 2: 12.2% weight loss at 13 weeks (oral tablet)
  • VANQUISH-1: Phase 3 enrollment completed (4,650 patients, 78 weeks)
  • Unique advantage: Only GLP-1/GIP dual agonist with advanced oral development

Evidence Strength: High — Strong Phase 2 data, Phase 3 enrollment complete, dual formulation pipeline.

Mechanism of Action

How VK2735 Works (Plain English)

VK2735 works similarly to tirzepatide (Mounjaro/Zepbound) by activating TWO hormone receptors instead of one:

  1. GLP-1 receptor activation — Reduces appetite, slows stomach emptying, improves blood sugar control. This is the same mechanism as Ozempic/Wegovy.

  2. GIP receptor activation — Enhances the GLP-1 effects AND directly improves how fat cells function. GIP receptors are found on fat tissue, potentially improving fat metabolism.

  3. Why two formulations matter — VK2735 is unique in having both injectable AND oral formulations in advanced development. The oral tablet showed strong efficacy (12.2% at 13 weeks), potentially offering an alternative for patients who prefer pills over injections.

Comparison to tirzepatide: Both are GLP-1/GIP dual agonists. The key differentiator for VK2735 is the oral formulation pipeline and Viking’s smaller company size (potential acquisition target).

Key Human Trials

VENTURE Phase 2 (Subcutaneous)

TrialDesignDurationN
VENTUREPhase 2, dose-ranging, placebo-controlled13 weeks175

Key Results:

  • Weight loss: Up to 14.7% at highest dose (vs 1.7% placebo)
  • Dose response: 9.1%, 10.9%, 12.9%, 14.7% across dose groups
  • No plateau: Weight loss curves still declining at week 13
  • Safety: Majority of AEs mild-moderate; well tolerated

VENTURE-Oral Phase 2 (Oral Tablet)

TrialDesignDurationN
VENTURE-OralPhase 2, dose-ranging13 weeks~160

Key Results:

  • Weight loss: Up to 12.2% at 13 weeks
  • ≥5% weight loss: 97% of treated participants (vs 10% placebo)
  • ≥10% weight loss: 80% of treated participants (vs 5% placebo)
  • Safety: 99% of AEs mild-moderate; no food restrictions required
