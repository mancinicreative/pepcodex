## Overview

Thymogen and Thymosin Alpha-1 are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Thymogen:** A synthetic dipeptide (glutamyl-tryptophan) developed in Russia as a defined successor to thymalin.

**Thymosin Alpha-1:** A 28-amino acid immunomodulatory peptide approved in over 35 countries including China and Italy for hepatitis B/C, cancer adjuvant therapy, and immunodeficiency.
