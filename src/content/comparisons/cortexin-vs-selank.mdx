## Overview

Cortexin and Selank are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Cortexin:** A Russian neuropeptide complex derived from porcine or bovine cerebral cortex, approved in Russia and CIS countries for neurological conditions.

**Selank:** A synthetic heptapeptide derived from the immunomodulatory peptide tuftsin, developed by Russian researchers and approved in Russia since 2009 for anxiety and neurasthenia.
