## Overview

<h2 id="overview" class="sr-only">Overview</h2>

### Quick Summary

- **First oral small-molecule GLP-1 agonist** (NOT a peptide — included for GLP-1 class completeness)
- **NDA submitted** to FDA for obesity indication (Priority Review)
- **ATTAIN-1:** 12.4% weight loss at 72 weeks (36mg dose)
- **No food restrictions** — can be taken anytime, unlike oral semaglutide (Rybelsus)
- **9,000+ patients** across ATTAIN (obesity) and ACHIEVE (T2D) Phase 3 programs
- **Convenient daily oral dosing** — alternative for injection-averse patients

**Evidence Strength: High** — Extensive Phase 3 data, NEJM publication, NDA submitted.

<h2 id="mechanism-of-action">Mechanism of Action</h2>

### How Orforglipron Works (Plain English)

Orforglipron achieves the same effect as injectable GLP-1 drugs like Ozempic, but in a pill form without being a peptide:

1. **Same target, different molecule** — Orforglipron is a small molecule that activates GLP-1 receptors just like semaglutide. But because it's not a peptide, it doesn't get destroyed by stomach acid and can be absorbed orally without special formulation tricks.

2. **No food restrictions** — Unlike oral semaglutide (Rybelsus), which requires fasting and water restrictions, orforglipron can be taken any time with or without food. This is because it's chemically different from peptides.

3. **Once daily, not weekly** — Orforglipron is taken daily (like a typical pill) rather than weekly. While this means more frequent dosing, some patients prefer daily routines.

**Why this matters:** For the millions of people who don't want injections, orforglipron offers a truly convenient oral option. The lack of food restrictions addresses a major limitation of Rybelsus.

<h2 id="key-human-trials">Key Human Trials</h2>

### ATTAIN Program (Obesity)

**ATTAIN-1:**
| Trial | Population | Duration | N |
|-------|------------|----------|---|
| ATTAIN-1 | BMI ≥30 or ≥27 with comorbidity | 72 weeks | ~1,000 |

**Key Results (NEJM):**
- **Weight loss (36mg):** -12.4% vs -2.2% placebo
- **≥10% weight loss:** 59.6% of participants
- **≥5% weight loss:** 77% of participants
- **Safety:** GI AEs consistent with injectable GLP-1 class

**ATTAIN-2 (Obesity + T2D):**
- **Weight loss (36mg):** -10.5% at 72 weeks
- **HbA1c reduction:** Significant improvement

**ATTAIN-MAINTAIN:**
- Demonstrated weight loss maintenance after switching from Wegovy/Zepbound

### ACHIEVE Program (Type 2 Diabetes)
