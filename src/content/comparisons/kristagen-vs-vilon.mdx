## Overview

Kristagen and Vilon are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Kristagen:** A synthetic tripeptide (Glu-Asp-Gly) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to regulate thymus function and support immune cell differentiation.

**Vilon:** A synthetic dipeptide (Lys-Glu) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson as part of the peptide bioregulation framework.
