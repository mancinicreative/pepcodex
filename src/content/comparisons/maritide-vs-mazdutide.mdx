## Overview

MariTide and Mazdutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**MariTide:** A bispecific peptide-antibody conjugate combining GLP-1 receptor agonism with GIP receptor antagonism.

**Mazdutide:** A dual GLP-1 and glucagon receptor agonist developed by Innovent/Eli Lilly.
