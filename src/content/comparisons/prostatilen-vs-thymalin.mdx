## Overview

Prostatilen and Thymalin are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Prostatilen:** A peptide complex extracted from bovine prostate glands, developed in Russia and registered as a pharmaceutical for prostatitis treatment.

**Thymalin:** A thymic peptide complex developed in Russia and used clinically in the former Soviet Union for immunomodulation.
