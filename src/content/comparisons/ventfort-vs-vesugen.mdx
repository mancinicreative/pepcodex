## Overview

Ventfort and Vesugen are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Ventfort:** A cytamin-class peptide supplement derived from blood vessel tissue, part of the Russian bioregulator framework.

**Vesugen:** A synthetic tripeptide (Lys-Glu-Asp) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to support vascular health by modulating endothelial gene expression.
