## Overview

Bronchogen and Thymalin are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Bronchogen:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp-Leu) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson for bronchial and respiratory tissue support.

**Thymalin:** A thymic peptide complex developed in Russia and used clinically in the former Soviet Union for immunomodulation.
