## Overview

Thymosin Alpha-1 and Vilon are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Thymosin Alpha-1:** A 28-amino acid immunomodulatory peptide approved in over 35 countries including China and Italy for hepatitis B/C, cancer adjuvant therapy, and immunodeficiency.

**Vilon:** A synthetic dipeptide (Lys-Glu) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson as part of the peptide bioregulation framework.
