## Overview

Epithalon and Ventfort are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Epithalon:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp-Gly) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to activate telomerase and extend lifespan.

**Ventfort:** A cytamin-class peptide supplement derived from blood vessel tissue, part of the Russian bioregulator framework.
