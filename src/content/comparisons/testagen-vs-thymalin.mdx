## Overview

Testagen and Thymalin are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Testagen:** A synthetic tripeptide developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to support testicular tissue and modulate Leydig cell function.

**Thymalin:** A thymic peptide complex developed in Russia and used clinically in the former Soviet Union for immunomodulation.
