## Overview

Thymosin Alpha-1 and Vladonix are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Thymosin Alpha-1:** A 28-amino acid immunomodulatory peptide approved in over 35 countries including China and Italy for hepatitis B/C, cancer adjuvant therapy, and immunodeficiency.

**Vladonix:** A cytamin-class peptide supplement derived from thymus tissue, part of the Russian bioregulator framework.
