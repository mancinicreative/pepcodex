## Overview

CagriSema and Semaglutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**CagriSema:** A fixed-dose combination of cagrilintide (amylin analog) and semaglutide (GLP-1 agonist) for chronic weight management.

**Semaglutide:** A GLP-1 receptor agonist FDA-approved for type 2 diabetes, obesity, cardiovascular risk reduction, and MASH.
