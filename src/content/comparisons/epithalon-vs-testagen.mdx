## Overview

Epithalon and Testagen are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Epithalon:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp-Gly) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to activate telomerase and extend lifespan.

**Testagen:** A synthetic tripeptide developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to support testicular tissue and modulate Leydig cell function.
