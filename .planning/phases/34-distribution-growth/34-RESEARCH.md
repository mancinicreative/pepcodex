# Phase 34: Distribution & Growth — Research

## Purpose

Solve the distribution bottleneck identified in the PMF Analysis (Audience Reach: 4/10, Brand Awareness: 3/10). PepCodex has 1,048+ pages of differentiated content that consumers desperately need, but no systematic approach to getting it in front of them.

## Current Distribution Channels

| Channel | Status | Performance |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| **Organic Search (SEO)** | Active (only channel) | Unknown — no analytics baseline yet |
| **Newsletter (Beehiiv)** | Active | Unknown — subscriber count, growth rate TBD |
| **Social Media** | None | No profiles, no presence |
| **Backlinks** | Unknown | No backlink audit performed |

| **Paid Acquisition** | None | No ad spend |

## SEO Technical Audit Scope

### Areas to Audit
1. **Crawlability**: Sitemap coverage, robots.txt, crawl budget
2. **Indexing**: How many of 1,048+ URLs are actually indexed?
3. **Internal Linking**: Link density, orphan pages, hub-spoke structure
4. **Canonical Tags**: Correct canonicals on comparison/calculator pages
5. **Page Speed**: Core Web Vitals (after v5.0 performance fixes)
6. **Mobile**: Responsive rendering, mobile usability errors
7. **Schema**: Rich snippet eligibility and appearance

### Key SEO Metrics to Baseline
- Total indexed pages (Search Console)
- Organic sessions/month
- Average position for target keywords
- Top 50 keywords by impressions
- Top 50 pages by organic clicks
- Comparison page indexing rate
- Dossier page indexing rate

## Programmatic SEO Strategy

### "[Peptide] + [Condition]" Pages
- Currently have: 15 condition hubs, 92 dossiers, 255 condition subpages
- Opportunity: Generate targeted landing pages for high-search combinations
- Example: "semaglutide for weight loss", "BPC-157 for injury recovery", "epithalon for anti-aging"
- Data source: Existing condition hub × peptide dossier cross-references
- Estimated new pages: 200-400 (depends on valid combinations)

### "[Peptide] vs [Peptide] for [Condition]" Pages
- Extension of comparison content
- Example: "semaglutide vs tirzepatide for weight loss"
- Very high search intent, very specific
- Can be generated from existing comparison + condition data

## Social Content Strategy

### Platform Analysis

| Platform | Audience Match | Content Format | Effort | Priority |
|----------|---------------|----------------|--------|----------|
| **Twitter/X** | Health researchers, biohackers, journalists | Threads, news commentary, data threads | Low | HIGH |
| **Instagram** | GLP-1 consumers, wellness audience | Carousels (comparison infographics), Reels | Medium | HIGH |
| **TikTok** | Younger GLP-1 curious, viral potential | Short explainers, myth-busting | Medium | MEDIUM |
| **LinkedIn** | Clinic owners, health professionals | Industry insights, regulatory updates | Low | MEDIUM |
| **YouTube** | Deep research audience | Long-form peptide explainers | High | LOW (defer) |
| **Reddit** | Biohackers, GLP-1 users | Value-add comments, AMAs | Low | HIGH |

### Content Formats (Reusable Templates)
1. **Comparison Carousel** — Side-by-side peptide comparison (Instagram/Twitter)
2. **Evidence Thread** — "What does the research actually say about [peptide]?" (Twitter)
3. **Regulatory Update** — "FDA just [did X]. Here's what it means." (All platforms)
4. **Myth Buster** — "No, [peptide] is NOT proven to [claim]" (TikTok/Instagram)
5. **Weekly Research Digest** — Top 3 peptide studies this week (Newsletter/Twitter)
6. **Comparison Verdict** — "Which is better: X vs Y? Here's what the evidence says" (All platforms)
7. **Safety Alert** — "[Peptide] safety profile — what you should know" (All platforms)

### Posting Cadence (Proposed)
- Twitter/X: 3-5x/week (threads, commentary, regulatory updates)
- Instagram: 3x/week (2 carousels, 1 Reel)
- Reddit: 2-3x/week (value-add comments in r/peptides, r/semaglutide, r/ozempic)
- Newsletter: 1x/week (already have blog content to repurpose)

## Backlink Strategy

### Target Categories

1. **Health Media** (authority backlinks)
   - STAT News, Endpoints News, FiercePharma
   - Pitch: Use PepCodex data/comparisons as source material

2. **Medical/Clinical Sites** (trust backlinks)
   - Functional medicine blogs, integrative health sites
   - Pitch: Link to PepCodex dossiers as patient education resource

3. **Wellness/Fitness Media** (traffic backlinks)
   - Men's Health, Women's Health, fitness publications
   - Pitch: Fact-check resource for peptide articles

4. **Academic/Educational** (authority backlinks)
   - University health education pages, research summaries
   - Pitch: Cite PepCodex evidence-graded content

### Outreach Template Themes
- "We built the most comprehensive peptide comparison database — here's what we found"
- "Your readers are asking about [peptide]. Here's our evidence-graded analysis"
- "Regulatory status tracker for peptides — a resource for your reporting"
