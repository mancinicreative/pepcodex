## Overview

Cagrilintide and Retatrutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Cagrilintide:** A long-acting amylin analog developed by Novo Nordisk for weight management.

**Retatrutide:** A triple GIP/GLP-1/glucagon receptor agonist in Phase 3 development.
