## Overview

Amycretin and Liraglutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Amycretin:** A novel single-molecule dual GLP-1/amylin receptor agonist developed by Novo Nordisk.

**Liraglutide:** The first long-acting GLP-1 receptor agonist, FDA-approved for type 2 diabetes (Victoza, 2010) and chronic weight management (Saxenda, 2014).
