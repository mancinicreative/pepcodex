## What is GHK-Cu?

GHK-Cu (glycyl-L-histidyl-L-lysine with copper) is a naturally occurring peptide in human blood that's widely used in skincare products for wound healing and anti-aging benefits.

## Key Facts

| Fact | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Natural** | Yes - found in human blood |
| **Primary Use** | Topical skincare |
| **Evidence Level** | Moderate (for topical use) |
| **Availability** | Legal in cosmetics |

## How Does It Work?

GHK-Cu helps with:
- Stimulating collagen production
- Promoting wound healing
- Supporting skin regeneration
- Providing antioxidant benefits (via copper)

## Where Is It Found?

GHK-Cu is a common ingredient in:
- Anti-aging serums and creams
- Post-procedure skincare
- Wound healing products
- Hair growth formulations

## Evidence Quality

Unlike many peptides, GHK-Cu has reasonable evidence for its topical uses:
- Multiple human studies on wound healing
- Cosmetic trials showing skin improvement
- Comparable to retinol and vitamin C in some studies

## What to Look For

When buying GHK-Cu products:
- Look for reputable brands
- Check concentration (typically 0.01-1%)
- Consider formulation stability
- Copper peptides need proper formulation
