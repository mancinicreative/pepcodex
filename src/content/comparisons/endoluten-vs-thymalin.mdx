## Overview

Endoluten and Thymalin are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Endoluten:** A cytamin-class peptide supplement derived from pineal gland tissue, part of the Russian bioregulator framework.

**Thymalin:** A thymic peptide complex developed in Russia and used clinically in the former Soviet Union for immunomodulation.
