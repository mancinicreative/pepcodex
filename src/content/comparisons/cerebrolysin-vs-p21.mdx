## Overview

Cerebrolysin and P21 are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Cerebrolysin:** A porcine brain-derived peptide mixture approved in over 50 countries (Austria, Russia, China, Germany) for stroke, dementia, and traumatic brain injury.

**P21:** A hexapeptide (Ac-DGGLAG-NH2) derived from ciliary neurotrophic factor (CNTF) that is claimed to mimic BDNF effects and promote neurogenesis.
