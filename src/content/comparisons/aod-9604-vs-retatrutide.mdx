## Overview

AOD-9604 and Retatrutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**AOD-9604:** A modified fragment of human growth hormone (amino acids 176-191 with N-terminal tyrosine) studied for fat loss.

**Retatrutide:** A triple GIP/GLP-1/glucagon receptor agonist in Phase 3 development.
