## Overview

Cagrilintide and Liraglutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Cagrilintide:** A long-acting amylin analog developed by Novo Nordisk for weight management.

**Liraglutide:** The first long-acting GLP-1 receptor agonist, FDA-approved for type 2 diabetes (Victoza, 2010) and chronic weight management (Saxenda, 2014).
