## Primary Research Sources

### PubMed / PMC
- **URL:** https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov / https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov
- **Use for:** Primary source for all citations, systematic reviews, meta-analyses

### ClinicalTrials.gov
- **URL:** https://clinicaltrials.gov
- **Use for:** Trial tracker page, finding active/recruiting trials, cancelled trial history

### Europe PMC
- **URL:** https://europepmc.org
- **Use for:** European trials and publications not always indexed on PubMed

- **URL:** https://www.usada.org
