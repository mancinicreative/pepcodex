## Overview

Epithalon and Pancragen are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Epithalon:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp-Gly) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to activate telomerase and extend lifespan.

**Pancragen:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Lys-Glu-Asp-Trp) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson for pancreatic support.
