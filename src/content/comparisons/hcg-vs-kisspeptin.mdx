## Overview

Human Chorionic Gonadotropin (hCG) and Kisspeptin are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Human Chorionic Gonadotropin (hCG):** A glycoprotein hormone FDA-approved for ovulation induction, cryptorchidism, and hypogonadotropic hypogonadism.

**Kisspeptin:** An endogenous neuropeptide that serves as the master regulator of reproductive function.
