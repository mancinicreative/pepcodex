## Overview

Stamakort and Thymalin are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Stamakort:** A peptide bioregulator developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson for gastric mucosa support.

**Thymalin:** A thymic peptide complex developed in Russia and used clinically in the former Soviet Union for immunomodulation.
