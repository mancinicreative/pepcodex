## Overview

CagriSema and Survodutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**CagriSema:** A fixed-dose combination of cagrilintide (amylin analog) and semaglutide (GLP-1 agonist) for chronic weight management.

**Survodutide:** A dual GLP-1/glucagon receptor agonist developed by Boehringer Ingelheim/Zealand Pharma, currently in Phase 3 trials for obesity and MASH.
