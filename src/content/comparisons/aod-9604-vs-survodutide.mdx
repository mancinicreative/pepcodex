## Overview

AOD-9604 and Survodutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**AOD-9604:** A modified fragment of human growth hormone (amino acids 176-191 with N-terminal tyrosine) studied for fat loss.

**Survodutide:** A dual GLP-1/glucagon receptor agonist developed by Boehringer Ingelheim/Zealand Pharma, currently in Phase 3 trials for obesity and MASH.
