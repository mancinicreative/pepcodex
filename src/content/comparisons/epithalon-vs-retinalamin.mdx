## Overview

Epithalon and Retinalamin are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Epithalon:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp-Gly) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to activate telomerase and extend lifespan.

**Retinalamin:** A peptide complex extracted from bovine or porcine retinal tissue, developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson for ophthalmologic applications.
