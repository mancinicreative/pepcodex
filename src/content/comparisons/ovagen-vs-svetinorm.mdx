## Overview

Ovagen and Svetinorm are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Ovagen:** A synthetic tripeptide (Glu-Asp-Leu) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson for liver support.

**Svetinorm:** A cytamin-class peptide supplement derived from liver tissue, part of the Russian bioregulator framework.
