## Overview

Amycretin and Retatrutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Amycretin:** A novel single-molecule dual GLP-1/amylin receptor agonist developed by Novo Nordisk.

**Retatrutide:** A triple GIP/GLP-1/glucagon receptor agonist in Phase 3 development.
