## Overview

Epithalon and Stamakort are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Epithalon:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp-Gly) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to activate telomerase and extend lifespan.

**Stamakort:** A peptide bioregulator developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson for gastric mucosa support.
