## Overview

Epithalon and Prostatilen are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Epithalon:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp-Gly) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to activate telomerase and extend lifespan.

**Prostatilen:** A peptide complex extracted from bovine prostate glands, developed in Russia and registered as a pharmaceutical for prostatitis treatment.
