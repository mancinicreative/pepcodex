## Overview

Chonluten and Epithalon are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Chonluten:** A synthetic tripeptide (Glu-Asp-Gly) developed by Vladimir Khavinson for gastrointestinal tissue support.

**Epithalon:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp-Gly) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to activate telomerase and extend lifespan.
