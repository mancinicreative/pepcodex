## Overview

Liraglutide and SLU-PP-332 are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Liraglutide:** The first long-acting GLP-1 receptor agonist, FDA-approved for type 2 diabetes (Victoza, 2010) and chronic weight management (Saxenda, 2014).

**SLU-PP-332:** A small molecule ERR (Estrogen-Related Receptor) agonist developed at Saint Louis University.
