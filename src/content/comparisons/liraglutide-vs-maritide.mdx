## Overview

Liraglutide and MariTide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Liraglutide:** The first long-acting GLP-1 receptor agonist, FDA-approved for type 2 diabetes (Victoza, 2010) and chronic weight management (Saxenda, 2014).

**MariTide:** A bispecific peptide-antibody conjugate combining GLP-1 receptor agonism with GIP receptor antagonism.
