## Overview

Liraglutide and Survodutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Liraglutide:** The first long-acting GLP-1 receptor agonist, FDA-approved for type 2 diabetes (Victoza, 2010) and chronic weight management (Saxenda, 2014).

**Survodutide:** A dual GLP-1/glucagon receptor agonist developed by Boehringer Ingelheim/Zealand Pharma, currently in Phase 3 trials for obesity and MASH.
