## Overview

Bronchogen and Epithalon are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Bronchogen:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp-Leu) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson for bronchial and respiratory tissue support.

**Epithalon:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp-Gly) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to activate telomerase and extend lifespan.
