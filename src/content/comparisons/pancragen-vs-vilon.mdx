## Overview

Pancragen and Vilon are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Pancragen:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Lys-Glu-Asp-Trp) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson for pancreatic support.

**Vilon:** A synthetic dipeptide (Lys-Glu) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson as part of the peptide bioregulation framework.
