## Literature Databases

### PubMed (NIH/NLM)
**URL:** https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov

**Best for:** Peer-reviewed articles on mechanisms, trials, case reports

**Search strategies:**
- Use MeSH terms and field tags: `ti:` (title), `tiab:` (title/abstract)
- Boolean operators: AND, OR, NOT
- Phrase matching with quotes

**Example queries:**
```
Epitalon telomerase human[ti]
"BPC-157" AND wound healing[tiab]
("peptide bioregulator" OR geroprotector) AND aging
semaglutide AND "randomized controlled trial"[pt]
```

---

### Europe PMC
**URL:** https://europepmc.org

**Best for:** Full-text search, preprints, open-access content (8.8M+ articles)

**Example queries:**
```
"thymalin lifespan" IN FULLTEXT
PREPRINT:"BCAA supplement exercise"
epithalon telomerase mechanism
```

---

### Cochrane Library
**URL:** https://www.cochranelibrary.com

**Best for:** Systematic reviews (gold-standard), CENTRAL trials registry

**Example queries:**
```
peptide bioregulator aging (Reviews)
"growth hormone releasing" (CENTRAL Trials)
```

---

### Google Scholar
**URL:** https://scholar.google.com

**Best for:** Catch-all including theses, books, foreign papers

**Trust: 3/5** - Indexes uncurated content. Always verify source credibility.

**Example queries:**
```
"пептидные биорегуляторы" site:elibrary.ru
"Epitalon Khavinson 2019"
```

## Trial Registries

### ClinicalTrials.gov
**URL:** https://clinicaltrials.gov

**Best for:** Registered interventional/observational studies (530K+)

**Example queries:**
```
Intervention: "BPC-157"
Condition: obesity AND Intervention: semaglutide
Status: Completed AND Has Results
```

### WHO ICTRP
**URL:** https://trialsearch.who.int

**Best for:** International trials (aggregates 20+ registries including EU-CTR, ChiCTR)

## Search Workflow

### For New Peptide Dossier
1. **PubMed:** peptide name + mechanism keywords
2. **ClinicalTrials.gov:** find trials, note NCT IDs
3. **Cochrane:** systematic reviews
4. **DrugBank/IUPHAR:** mechanism details
5. **eLibrary.ru:** Russian bioregulator research
6. **Retraction Watch:** verify key citations
7. **FDA/EMA:** regulatory status

### For Russian Peptide Research
1. **eLibrary.ru** in Russian
2. **CyberLeninka** for open access
3. **Key author:** Khavinson V.Kh. (Хавинсон В.Х.)
4. **Key journal:** Advances in Gerontology

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
