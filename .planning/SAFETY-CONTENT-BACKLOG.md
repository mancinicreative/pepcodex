## Content Strategy

### Sprint Planning

- **Sprint 1**: Articles 1-5 (high-profile individual profiles)
- **Sprint 2**: Articles 6-10 + Article 13 (cognitive overview)
- **Sprint 3**: Articles 11-12 + Articles 17-20 (topic guides)
- **Sprint 4**: Articles 14-16 (class overviews for new agents)
- **Sprint 5+**: Tier 2 articles as bandwidth allows

### Quality Standards

Each safety article must include:
- [ ] Minimum 4 cited sources (PubMed/regulatory)
- [ ] Known side effects from clinical data
- [ ] Drug interaction warnings (if applicable)
- [ ] Regulatory status (FDA/EMA/other)
- [ ] Clear disclaimers
- [ ] Evidence grading (high/moderate/low/very-low)

### Template Structure

```
1. Overview & Mechanism
2. Clinical Safety Data (human studies)
3. Known Side Effects (common, serious, rare)
4. Drug Interactions
5. Special Populations (contraindications)
6. Regulatory Status
7. Key Safety Studies (cited)
8. Disclaimer
```
