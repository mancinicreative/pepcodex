## Overview

GHRP-2 and MK-677 are both studied in the peptide research space.

**GHRP-2:** A synthetic hexapeptide GH secretagogue with the strongest GH-releasing potency in its class.

**MK-677:** An orally-active, non-peptide growth hormone secretagogue that mimics ghrelin's effects on GHS-R1a.
