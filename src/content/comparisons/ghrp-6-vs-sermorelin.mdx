## Overview

GHRP-6 and Sermorelin are both studied in the peptide research space.

**GHRP-6:** A first-generation growth hormone secretagogue that stimulates GH release through the ghrelin receptor.

**Sermorelin:** A GHRH analog that was FDA-approved for pediatric GH deficiency.
