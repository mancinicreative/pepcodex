## What Is PepCodex

**The citation-backed peptide research library**

PepCodex synthesizes peer-reviewed studies from PubMed, clinical trials, and global literature into comprehensive dossiers. Every claim is citation-backed. No dosing protocols, no sourcing info—just evidence.

**Why it exists:** Peptide research is scattered across journals, buried in paywalls, and mixed with misinformation. We make it accessible, graded, and trustworthy.
