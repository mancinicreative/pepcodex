## Overview

Dihexa and Selank are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Dihexa:** An angiotensin IV analog developed by Washington State University researchers, claimed to enhance cognition through HGF/c-Met signaling.

**Selank:** A synthetic heptapeptide derived from the immunomodulatory peptide tuftsin, developed by Russian researchers and approved in Russia since 2009 for anxiety and neurasthenia.
