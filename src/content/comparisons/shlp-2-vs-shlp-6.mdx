## Overview

SHLP-2 and SHLP-6 are both studied in the peptide research space.

**SHLP-2:** A 26-amino acid mitochondria-derived peptide (MDP) discovered in 2016 from the same mitochondrial DNA region as humanin.

**SHLP-6:** A 22-amino acid mitochondria-derived peptide with the unique property of being pro-apoptotic—the opposite of its sister peptides humanin and SHLP-2.
