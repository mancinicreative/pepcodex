## Overview

FOXO4-DRI and SHLP-6 are both studied in the peptide research space.

**FOXO4-DRI:** A D-retro-inverso peptide designed to disrupt the FOXO4-p53 interaction, selectively eliminating senescent cells (senolytic).

**SHLP-6:** A 22-amino acid mitochondria-derived peptide with the unique property of being pro-apoptotic—the opposite of its sister peptides humanin and SHLP-2.
