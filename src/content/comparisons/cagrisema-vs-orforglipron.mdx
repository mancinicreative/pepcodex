## Overview

CagriSema and Orforglipron are both studied in the peptide research space.

**CagriSema:** A fixed-dose combination of cagrilintide (amylin analog) and semaglutide (GLP-1 agonist) for chronic weight management.

**Orforglipron:** The first oral small-molecule (non-peptide) GLP-1 receptor agonist.
