## Overview

Livagen and Svetinorm are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Livagen:** A synthetic tripeptide (Lys-Glu-Asp) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to support liver function and modulate hepatic gene expression.

**Svetinorm:** A cytamin-class peptide supplement derived from liver tissue, part of the Russian bioregulator framework.
