## Overview

Amycretin and Mazdutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Amycretin:** A novel single-molecule dual GLP-1/amylin receptor agonist developed by Novo Nordisk.

**Mazdutide:** A dual GLP-1 and glucagon receptor agonist developed by Innovent/Eli Lilly.
