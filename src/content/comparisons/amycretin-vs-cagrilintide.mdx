## Overview

Amycretin and Cagrilintide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Amycretin:** A novel single-molecule dual GLP-1/amylin receptor agonist developed by Novo Nordisk.

**Cagrilintide:** A long-acting amylin analog developed by Novo Nordisk for weight management.
