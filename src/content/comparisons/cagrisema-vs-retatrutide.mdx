## Overview

CagriSema and Retatrutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**CagriSema:** A fixed-dose combination of cagrilintide (amylin analog) and semaglutide (GLP-1 agonist) for chronic weight management.

**Retatrutide:** A triple GIP/GLP-1/glucagon receptor agonist in Phase 3 development.
