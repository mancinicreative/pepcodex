## Overview

MariTide and Orforglipron are both studied in the peptide research space.

**MariTide:** A bispecific peptide-antibody conjugate combining GLP-1 receptor agonism with GIP receptor antagonism.

**Orforglipron:** The first oral small-molecule (non-peptide) GLP-1 receptor agonist.
