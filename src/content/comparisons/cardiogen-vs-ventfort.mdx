## Overview

Cardiogen and Ventfort are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Cardiogen:** A synthetic tripeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson for cardiac tissue support.

**Ventfort:** A cytamin-class peptide supplement derived from blood vessel tissue, part of the Russian bioregulator framework.
