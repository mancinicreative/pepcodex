## Overview

Livagen and Vilon are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Livagen:** A synthetic tripeptide (Lys-Glu-Asp) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to support liver function and modulate hepatic gene expression.

**Vilon:** A synthetic dipeptide (Lys-Glu) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson as part of the peptide bioregulation framework.
