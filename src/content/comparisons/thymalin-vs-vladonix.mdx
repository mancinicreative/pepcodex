## Overview

Thymalin and Vladonix are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Thymalin:** A thymic peptide complex developed in Russia and used clinically in the former Soviet Union for immunomodulation.

**Vladonix:** A cytamin-class peptide supplement derived from thymus tissue, part of the Russian bioregulator framework.
