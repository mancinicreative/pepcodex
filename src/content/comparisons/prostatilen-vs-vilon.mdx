## Overview

Prostatilen and Vilon are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Prostatilen:** A peptide complex extracted from bovine prostate glands, developed in Russia and registered as a pharmaceutical for prostatitis treatment.

**Vilon:** A synthetic dipeptide (Lys-Glu) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson as part of the peptide bioregulation framework.
