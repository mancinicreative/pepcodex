## Overview

Pancragen and Suprefort are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Pancragen:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Lys-Glu-Asp-Trp) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson for pancreatic support.

**Suprefort:** A cytamin-class peptide supplement derived from pancreatic tissue, part of the Russian bioregulator framework.
