# Phase 32: Monetization Foundation — Research

## Purpose

Build revenue infrastructure on top of PepCodex's content moat. Current monetization score: 2/10. Target: operational revenue channels even if initial revenue is $0 while traffic grows.

## Revenue Model Benchmarks

### Examine.com (Closest Comparable)
- **Model**: Free content + premium membership ($29/mo or $199/yr)
- **Revenue**: $700K+/yr (estimated from ConvertFlow case study)
- **Traffic**: Millions of monthly visitors
- **Moat**: Evidence-based supplement database
- **Lesson**: PepCodex has MORE content depth on peptides than Examine has on supplements. Same trust model works.

### Peptides.org (Direct Competitor)
- **Model**: Vendor affiliate reviews + display ads
- **Revenue**: Unknown (likely substantial from vendor partnerships)
- **Weakness**: Trust conflict — "trusted resource" that monetizes via vendor reviews
- **Lesson**: PepCodex should NOT do vendor affiliates. The no-conflict positioning IS the moat.

### Health Media Revenue Ranges
| Channel | Low | High | Notes |
|---------|-----|------|-------|
| Clinic directory listings | $99/mo | $499/mo | Per clinic, tiered by features |

## CTA Strategy

### Comparison Pages (279 pages — highest-intent content)
- Users searching "semaglutide vs tirzepatide" are decision-stage
- CTA options:
  1. **"Talk to a peptide specialist"** → Clinic directory referral
  2. **"Get weekly peptide research updates"** → Newsletter signup
  3. **"Download this comparison as PDF"** → Lead magnet / email capture
- Placement: After verdict section, before FAQs

### Condition Hub Pages (15 pages)
- Users searching "peptides for weight loss" are research-stage
- CTA options:
  1. **"Find a clinic near you"** → City page / clinic directory
  2. **"Which peptide matches your goals?"** → Quiz funnel
  3. **"Subscribe for [condition] research updates"** → Segmented newsletter

### Dossier Pages (92 pages)
- Deep researchers, highest-value audience
- CTA: Subtle newsletter signup, not aggressive monetization
- Trust preservation is paramount on dossier pages

## Clinic Partnership Model

### Tiers (Proposed)

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Basic** | $99/mo | Listing in clinic directory, basic profile |
| **Standard** | $249/mo | Featured listing, city page placement, logo display |
| **Premium** | $499/mo | All Standard + patient education content license, priority placement |

### Clinic Value Proposition
- PepCodex audience = pre-qualified patients searching for peptide information
- No cold leads — these are people already researching peptides
- Education-first positioning builds patient trust before clinic visit
- Monthly reporting on impressions and referral clicks

## PepCodex Pro Concept

### Target Audience
- Peptide clinics, functional medicine practitioners, telehealth platforms
- Need patient education materials they can trust
- Currently using generic resources or creating their own

### Deliverables (Proposed)
- White-label dossier PDFs (branded for clinic)
- Monthly research digest
- Regulatory status updates
- Patient FAQ sheets per peptide
- Early access to new dossiers/comparisons

### Pricing (Proposed)
- **Solo Practitioner**: $199/mo
- **Multi-Location Clinic**: $499/mo
- **Enterprise/Telehealth**: Custom ($1,000+/mo)

## Quiz Funnel Design

### "Which peptide research matches your interest?"
Flow:
1. What's your primary interest? (weight management / recovery / anti-aging / cognitive / immune)
2. How familiar are you with peptide research? (just learning / intermediate / advanced)
3. What matters most to you? (evidence quality / safety data / comparison info / regulatory status)
4. Results page → Personalized content recommendations + newsletter signup + nearest clinic suggestion

### Conversion Targets
- Quiz completion rate: 60%+
- Email capture rate: 40%+
- Clinic referral click rate: 15%+
