# 40 Years of Peptide Therapeutics: 1985-2025

A chronological history of peptide drug development, FDA approvals, and scientific milestones.

---

## Pre-1985: Foundation Era

| Year | Milestone | Significance |
|------|-----------|--------------|
| **1882** | Curtius synthesizes benzoylglycylglycine | Birth of peptide chemistry |
| **1901** | Fischer coins term "peptide" | First free dipeptide synthesized |
| **1921** | Banting isolates insulin | First therapeutic peptide identified |
| **1922** | Insulin enters clinical use | First peptide drug (animal-derived) |
| **1923** | Commercial insulin production | First commercial peptide drug |
| **1928** | Vasopressin discovered | Antidiuretic hormone identified |
| **1951** | Vasopressin sequence elucidated | 9 amino acid structure determined |
| **1954** | Du Vigneaud synthesizes oxytocin & vasopressin | **Nobel Prize 1955** |
| **1963** | Merrifield invents SPPS | Solid-phase peptide synthesis revolutionizes field |
| **1983** | Cyclosporine approved (Sandimmune) | First cyclic peptide immunosuppressant |

## 1985-1989: The GnRH Era Begins

| Year | Drug | Brand | Indication | Notes |
|------|------|-------|------------|-------|
| **1985** | Recombinant human insulin | Humulin | Diabetes | First recombinant peptide drug |
| **1985** | Leuprolide | Lupron | Prostate cancer | First GnRH agonist; daily SC injection |
| **1987** | Octreotide | Sandostatin | Acromegaly, carcinoid | Somatostatin analog; 8 amino acids |
| **1988** | Desmopressin | DDAVP | Diabetes insipidus, bleeding disorders | Vasopressin analog |
| **1989** | Goserelin | Zoladex | Prostate/breast cancer | First depot implant peptide |
| **1989** | Leuprolide depot | Lupron Depot | Prostate cancer | Monthly IM formulation |

**Key Trend:** GnRH analogs validate peptide drug market viability

## Evolution of Key Technologies

### Half-Life Extension Strategies
| Era | Strategy | Example | Half-life |
|-----|----------|---------|-----------|
| 1980s | Native peptide | Leuprolide (daily) | Hours |
| 1990s | Depot formulations | Lupron Depot (monthly) | Weeks |
| 2000s | Exenatide ER (weekly) | Days | Weekly |
| 2010s | Fatty acid acylation | Liraglutide, Semaglutide | Days-week |
| 2010s | Fc fusion | Dulaglutide | ~5 days |
| 2020s | Antibody conjugate | MariTide (monthly) | ~21 days |

### Route of Administration Evolution
| Era | Dominant Route | Breakthrough |
|-----|---------------|--------------|
| 1980s | SC injection, IM depot | Depot implants |
| 1990s | SC, IM, nasal | Nasal spray peptides |
| 2000s | SC, IT | Intrathecal (ziconotide) |
| 2010s | SC weekly | Once-weekly formulations |
| 2019 | **Oral peptide** | Rybelsus (oral semaglutide) |
| 2020s | Oral small molecule | Orforglipron |

## Therapeutic Area Evolution

### 1985-1995: Hormone Replacement
- GnRH agonists (cancer)
- Somatostatin analogs (acromegaly)
- Calcitonin (osteoporosis)

### 1995-2005: Cardiovascular & HIV
- Anticoagulants (bivalirudin, eptifibatide)
- HIV fusion inhibitors (enfuvirtide)
- Pain management (ziconotide)

### 2005-2015: Metabolic Revolution
- GLP-1 agonists (diabetes → obesity)
- Amylin analogs (pramlintide)
- GHRH analogs (tesamorelin)

### 2015-2025: Multi-Target & Precision
- Dual/triple agonists (tirzepatide, retatrutide)
- PRRT (Lutathera, Pluvicto)
- Personalized vaccines (mRNA-4157)
- Mitochondrial peptides (elamipretide)
- Bicycle drug conjugates (zelenectide pevedotin)
