## Overview

MOTS-c and SHLP-2 are both studied in the peptide research space.

**MOTS-c:** A 16-amino acid mitochondrial-derived peptide encoded in mtDNA that targets AMPK/mTOR pathways for metabolic regulation.

**SHLP-2:** A 26-amino acid mitochondria-derived peptide (MDP) discovered in 2016 from the same mitochondrial DNA region as humanin.
