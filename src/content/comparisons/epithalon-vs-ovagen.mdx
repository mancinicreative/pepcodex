## Overview

Epithalon and Ovagen are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Epithalon:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp-Gly) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to activate telomerase and extend lifespan.

**Ovagen:** A synthetic tripeptide (Glu-Asp-Leu) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson for liver support.
