## Objection Handling

### "You don't have traffic yet"

"Fair point. That's exactly why we're offering Founding Partner pricing—50% off locked for 12 months. You're getting in early before rates increase. We're investing heavily in SEO and already have 188 pages indexed. Happy to share a traffic update in 60 days."

### "How do we measure ROI?"

"Great question. Verified listings include a basic analytics dashboard showing views and clicks. We can also set up UTM tracking so you see exactly which leads come from PepCodex."

### "We're concerned about brand safety"

"Totally understand. We never give dosing, protocols, or sourcing info. Every claim is citation-backed. We have a strict editorial policy. Sponsors have zero editorial influence—that's in writing."

### "We need to think about it"

"Of course. To help with the decision—Founding Partner pricing ends Feb 28. After that, it's standard rates. Want me to hold a spot for you until then?"

## CRM Pipeline Stages

1. **Prospect** — Added to list, not contacted
2. **Contacted** — First email sent
3. **Replied** — Got a response (positive, negative, or question)
4. **Call Scheduled** — Discovery call booked
5. **Proposal Sent** — Sent pricing/details
6. **Negotiating** — Back and forth on terms
7. **Closed Won** — Signed up
8. **Closed Lost** — Said no (note reason)
9. **Nurture** — Not now, follow up later
