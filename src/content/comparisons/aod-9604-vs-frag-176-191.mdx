## Overview

AOD-9604 and HGH Fragment 176-191 are both derived from the C-terminal region of human growth hormone, marketed for fat loss. They share a nearly identical sequence but AOD-9604 has been through more formal clinical development. Neither is FDA-approved for any indication.

**Important clarification:** These peptides are often confused or treated as identical. While closely related, AOD-9604 underwent clinical trials while "Fragment 176-191" typically refers to research chemical products.
