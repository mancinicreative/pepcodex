## Overview

CagriSema and MariTide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**CagriSema:** A fixed-dose combination of cagrilintide (amylin analog) and semaglutide (GLP-1 agonist) for chronic weight management.

**MariTide:** A bispecific peptide-antibody conjugate combining GLP-1 receptor agonism with GIP receptor antagonism.
