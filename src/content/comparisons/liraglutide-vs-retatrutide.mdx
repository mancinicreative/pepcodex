## Overview

Liraglutide and Retatrutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Liraglutide:** The first long-acting GLP-1 receptor agonist, FDA-approved for type 2 diabetes (Victoza, 2010) and chronic weight management (Saxenda, 2014).

**Retatrutide:** A triple GIP/GLP-1/glucagon receptor agonist in Phase 3 development.
