## Overview

MOTS-c and SHLP-6 are both studied in the peptide research space.

**MOTS-c:** A 16-amino acid mitochondrial-derived peptide encoded in mtDNA that targets AMPK/mTOR pathways for metabolic regulation.

**SHLP-6:** A 22-amino acid mitochondria-derived peptide with the unique property of being pro-apoptotic—the opposite of its sister peptides humanin and SHLP-2.
