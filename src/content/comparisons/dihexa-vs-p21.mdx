## Overview

Dihexa and P21 are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Dihexa:** An angiotensin IV analog developed by Washington State University researchers, claimed to enhance cognition through HGF/c-Met signaling.

**P21:** A hexapeptide (Ac-DGGLAG-NH2) derived from ciliary neurotrophic factor (CNTF) that is claimed to mimic BDNF effects and promote neurogenesis.
