## Mechanism of Action

Stamakort is proposed to support gastric mucosa through peptide bioregulation mechanisms developed by Vladimir Khavinson.

### Proposed Mechanisms

1. **Mucosal Gene Modulation** - Claimed to regulate gene expression in gastric mucosa cells
2. **Protective Function** - Proposed effects on stomach lining integrity
3. **Digestive Support** - Claimed involvement in gastric secretory function

### Important Limitations

- No Western clinical validation or regulatory approval
- Limited Russian preclinical data only
- Amino acid sequence not fully characterized in public literature
- NOT a treatment for ulcers or gastric conditions
- No controlled human clinical trials
