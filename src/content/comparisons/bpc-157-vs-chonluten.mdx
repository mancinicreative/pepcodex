## Overview

BPC-157 and Chonluten are both studied in the peptide research space.

**BPC-157:** A synthetic peptide derived from human gastric juice protein.

**Chonluten:** A synthetic tripeptide (Glu-Asp-Gly) developed by Vladimir Khavinson for gastrointestinal tissue support.
