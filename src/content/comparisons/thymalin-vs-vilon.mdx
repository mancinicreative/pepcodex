## Overview

Thymalin and Vilon are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Thymalin:** A thymic peptide complex developed in Russia and used clinically in the former Soviet Union for immunomodulation.

**Vilon:** A synthetic dipeptide (Lys-Glu) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson as part of the peptide bioregulation framework.
