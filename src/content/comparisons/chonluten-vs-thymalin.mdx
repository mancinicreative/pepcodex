## Overview

Chonluten and Thymalin are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Chonluten:** A synthetic tripeptide (Glu-Asp-Gly) developed by Vladimir Khavinson for gastrointestinal tissue support.

**Thymalin:** A thymic peptide complex developed in Russia and used clinically in the former Soviet Union for immunomodulation.
