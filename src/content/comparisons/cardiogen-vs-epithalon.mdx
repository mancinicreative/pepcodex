## Overview

Cardiogen and Epithalon are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Cardiogen:** A synthetic tripeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson for cardiac tissue support.

**Epithalon:** A synthetic tetrapeptide (Ala-Glu-Asp-Gly) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to activate telomerase and extend lifespan.
