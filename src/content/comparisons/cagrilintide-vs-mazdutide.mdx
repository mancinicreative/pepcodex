## Overview

Cagrilintide and Mazdutide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Cagrilintide:** A long-acting amylin analog developed by Novo Nordisk for weight management.

**Mazdutide:** A dual GLP-1 and glucagon receptor agonist developed by Innovent/Eli Lilly.
