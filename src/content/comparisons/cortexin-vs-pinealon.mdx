## Overview

Cortexin and Pinealon are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Cortexin:** A Russian neuropeptide complex derived from porcine or bovine cerebral cortex, approved in Russia and CIS countries for neurological conditions.

**Pinealon:** A synthetic tripeptide (Glu-Asp-Arg) developed by Russian researcher Vladimir Khavinson as a 'peptide bioregulator' claimed to support brain function.
