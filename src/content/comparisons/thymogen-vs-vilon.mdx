## Overview

Thymogen and Vilon are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Thymogen:** A synthetic dipeptide (glutamyl-tryptophan) developed in Russia as a defined successor to thymalin.

**Vilon:** A synthetic dipeptide (Lys-Glu) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson as part of the peptide bioregulation framework.
