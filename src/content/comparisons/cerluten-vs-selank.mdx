## Overview

Cerluten and Selank are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Cerluten:** A cytamin-class peptide supplement derived from brain tissue, part of the Russian bioregulator framework.

**Selank:** A synthetic heptapeptide derived from the immunomodulatory peptide tuftsin, developed by Russian researchers and approved in Russia since 2009 for anxiety and neurasthenia.
