## Overview

GHRP-6 and MK-677 are both studied in the peptide research space.

**GHRP-6:** A first-generation growth hormone secretagogue that stimulates GH release through the ghrelin receptor.

**MK-677:** An orally-active, non-peptide growth hormone secretagogue that mimics ghrelin's effects on GHS-R1a.
