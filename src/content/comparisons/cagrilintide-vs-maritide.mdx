## Overview

Cagrilintide and MariTide are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Cagrilintide:** A long-acting amylin analog developed by Novo Nordisk for weight management.

**MariTide:** A bispecific peptide-antibody conjugate combining GLP-1 receptor agonism with GIP receptor antagonism.
