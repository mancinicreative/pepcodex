## Mechanism of Action

Ventfort is a cytamin-class supplement containing peptide complexes derived from blood vessel tissue.

### Proposed Mechanisms

1. **Endothelial Support** - Claimed to support blood vessel lining function
2. **Vascular Tone** - Proposed effects on vessel elasticity
3. **Cardiovascular Health** - Marketed for overall vascular support

### Important Limitations

- NOT a defined peptide - contains complex mixture
- Oral peptide bioavailability is generally very low
- No Western clinical validation
- No randomized controlled trials
- NOT a treatment for cardiovascular disease
- Not approved by any Western regulatory agency
