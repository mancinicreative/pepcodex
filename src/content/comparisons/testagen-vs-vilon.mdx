## Overview

Testagen and Vilon are both studied in the peptide research space.

**Testagen:** A synthetic tripeptide developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson, claimed to support testicular tissue and modulate Leydig cell function.

**Vilon:** A synthetic dipeptide (Lys-Glu) developed by Russian scientist Vladimir Khavinson as part of the peptide bioregulation framework.
