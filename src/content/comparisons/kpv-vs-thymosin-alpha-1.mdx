## Overview

KPV and Thymosin Alpha-1 are both studied in the peptide research space.

**KPV:** A naturally occurring tripeptide derived from the C-terminus of alpha-melanocyte-stimulating hormone (alpha-MSH).

**Thymosin Alpha-1:** A 28-amino acid immunomodulatory peptide approved in over 35 countries including China and Italy for hepatitis B/C, cancer adjuvant therapy, and immunodeficiency.
